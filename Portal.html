Vifo Merit Excellence Award 2026

● Open Deadline: June 15, 2026

The Vifo Merit Excellence Award recognises outstanding students who demonstrate exceptional commitment, discipline, and potential. This scholarship covers tuition and provides a monthly stipend for the full programme duration.

$2,400 / year

12 months (renewable)

3 Students

Eligibility

  • Enrolled in Vifo for a minimum of 3 months
  • Consistent attendance and lesson completion
  • Good standing — no disciplinary record
Admin Note: To apply, send a message with the subject "Scholarship Application — Merit Excellence Award 2026" and include a short paragraph about why you deserve this scholarship. All applications are reviewed personally.

International Access Bursary

● Open Deadline: July 1, 2026

The International Access Bursary supports students from regions with limited access to quality financial and English education. Awarded based on financial need and demonstrated motivation — partial or full fee waivers available.

Up to 100% fee waiver

6 months

5 Students

Eligibility

  • Demonstrated financial need (brief statement required)
  • Active participation in at least one module
  • Clear learning goals and strong motivation
Admin Note: If financial barriers are in the way of your learning, please reach out. Send a message titled "Bursary Application — International Access" and share your situation honestly. This is close to my heart.
